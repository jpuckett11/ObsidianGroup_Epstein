    ABOUT US

    Aviatri was founded by a female pilot, Nadia Marcinko, whose passion for aviation moved her to help set the stage for more women to take advantage of the exciting career opportunities in aviation. After a successful career as a high fashion model, Nadia was ready for longer runways and became a licensed flight instructor, aerobatic pilot and an Airline Transport Pilot (ATP), rated on Gulfstream II, III, IV and multiple Boeing aircraft. During her time as a pilot, Nadia developed a thorough understanding of the aviation industry and its demands. Since 2011 Nadia has been sharing her expertise as a spokesperson for aviation and through consulting work at her company, Aviloop, which develops creative marketing strategies to help accelerate the growth of other aviation businesses. Nadia's love for her profession fueled her vision for a platform, where women could discover aviation as a potential career, get help obtaining their training and find a dream job. Today, Aviatri is the only pilot agency that focuses specifically on female clients and their needs.
