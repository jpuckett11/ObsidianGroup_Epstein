# CONTINUITY: The Pattern Successor Hypothesis

## Category framing

This file opens a new category in the OWG investigation: **CONTINUITY**. The category investigates the structural hypothesis that the Epstein network did not end with Epstein's death in August 2019. It investigates instead whether the financial-influence pattern Epstein operated has been picked up by current actors, in particular the Trump family and Jared Kushner, with similar movements and dealings, similar sovereign-fund concentrations, and the same institutional counterparties.

This is investigative hypothesis, not accusation. Each finding below is documented from public-record sources or from the Department of Justice's Epstein Files Transparency Act (EFTA) release. Gaps and "we do not know yet" markers are stated explicitly throughout.

Methodologically, the test is the one Jay Puckett articulated 2026-06-12: identify named payers and institutional counterparties from the Epstein era, then check whether the same entities, the same principals, or close-circle successors are now paying or institutionally connected to the Trump family and Jared Kushner. A documented overlap is the hypothesis-supporting evidence. Absence of overlap is hypothesis-disconfirming. Both directions are recorded honestly.

## Headline finding: Apollo (Leon Black) to Marc Rowan to the Gaza governance board

The single most documentable institutional-continuity thread between the Epstein era and the present Trump network runs through Apollo Global Management.

### The Epstein-era documentation

Apollo Global Management co-founder **Leon Black** paid Jeffrey Epstein **$158 million between 2012 and 2017** for personal tax and estate planning advice. This is the finding of the independent review conducted by the Dechert law firm in 2021, commissioned by Apollo's own board. The figure is uncontested. The relationship was the proximate cause of Black's January 2021 departure as Apollo's CEO and Chairman.

OWG vault material additionally documents Apollo as the central node in a broader money laundering and political-influence network. See [[1MDB-Barr-PHUN-China Laundering Network]] for the full structural map, including the Apollo and Black relationships with Trump's casinos via Milken-era junk bonds, the Environmental Solutions Worldwide (ESWW) "money laundering vehicle" finding, the Apollo and Moscow connections, the 666 Fifth Avenue (Kushner) bailout role, and the Treasury-Mnuchin overlap.

### The Trump-era documentation

**Marc Rowan**, current CEO of Apollo Global Management and a Milken protege named in the same OWG network analysis as Black and Josh Harris, emerged as a **top contender for Treasury Secretary** in the second Trump administration. Per Financial Times reporting cited in November 2024 coverage, Rowan rushed back from Hong Kong to meet Trump in Florida to pitch for the role. Other candidates considered included Scott Bessent (who ultimately got the post), Kevin Warsh, and Bill Hagerty. Rowan was actively considered and was personally vetted by Trump.

Following the Treasury Secretary path closing, Trump appointed Rowan to the **Board of Peace**, the governance body whose founding charter was signed at the World Economic Forum in Davos in January 2026 to oversee Gaza's transition and reconstruction. Per Al Jazeera (Jan 16, 2026), Euronews (Jan 17, 2026), and CBS News reporting, the Board of Peace's founding Executive Board comprises:

- **Marco Rubio** (Secretary of State)
- **Steve Witkoff** (US Special Envoy for the Middle East)
- **Jared Kushner** (Trump son-in-law)
- **Tony Blair** (former UK Prime Minister)
- **Marc Rowan** (CEO, Apollo Global Management)
- **Ajay Banga** (World Bank Group President)
- **Robert Gabriel** (National Security Adviser)

Separately, **Nickolay Mladenov** (Bulgarian diplomat and former senior UN official) was named as **High Representative for Gaza**, a role parallel to but distinct from the Board of Peace.

This is the institutional continuity at its sharpest: the firm that paid Epstein $158 million has its current Chief Executive sitting on a Trump-appointed governance body alongside Trump's son-in-law, Trump's most trusted Mideast operator, and Trump's National Security Adviser. The Board of Peace will direct multi-billion-dollar Gulf-state Gaza reconstruction money flows in the coming years. The hands receiving that money belong to a network whose Epstein-era documentation is in the public Dechert review and in this investigation's vault.

### What this does not prove

It does not prove that Rowan personally consulted Epstein. The Dechert review specifically found no evidence Rowan or Josh Harris had personal Epstein business relationships. The continuity here is institutional, not personal. Apollo as an institution paid Epstein $158M. Apollo's current CEO is on a Trump governance board with Kushner and Witkoff. Whether the institutional continuity carries personal continuity is a question this finding raises, not one it answers.

### What it does establish

Apollo as an institution has not been broken up or sanctioned for the Epstein relationship. Its operating leadership has migrated, via the same political-access pattern, into the governance of the next major Gulf-money-handling structure (Gaza reconstruction). The institutional pipe is intact; the labels at each end have changed. This is the cleanest single documentable example of the continuity hypothesis to date.

## Affinity Partners: Kushner's Saudi PIF override

Six months after leaving the White House at the end of Trump's first term, Jared Kushner secured a **$2 billion investment from Saudi Arabia's Public Investment Fund (PIF)**. The PIF's own investment committee recommended rejecting the proposal, citing "the inexperience of the Affinity Fund management" and "excessive" fee structure. The committee's recommendation was **overruled by Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman**, who heads PIF's Board of Directors.

This is the same structural pattern Epstein operated with Leslie Wexner: principal grants total discretion to American intermediary, normal due-diligence process is bypassed, conventional advisory disagreement is overridden at the top, the American intermediary gets discretion over capital that no normal process would have approved.

## Saudi PIF: same-payer continuity, not successor pattern

Saudi Public Investment Fund is documented in this investigation as the cleanest example of **same-payer continuity across the Epstein and Trump-administration eras**. PIF is not a successor to an Epstein payer; PIF is the same payer continuously across both eras.

### Epstein-era activity

PIF was active during Epstein's operational period. Mohammed bin Salman was meeting US elites in New York during the period 2016-2019. Direct PIF payments to Epstein are not in the documented public record found here. The MBS-Kushner relationship was already operational pre-2019.

### Current Trump-era activity

- **$2 billion to Affinity Partners** (Kushner), with PIF investment committee rejection overridden by MBS
- **LIV Golf**, including hosting fees paid to Trump golf courses for tournaments. PIF announced in April 2026 that it will end LIV funding after the 2026 season. Bloomberg reports LIV is taking on advisers to find new investors after PIF exit (May 2026).
- Continuing direct diplomatic, real-estate, and commercial deal flow with the Trump administration

### Structural reading

PIF is the persistent payer; its American counterparties have changed (from Epstein-network operators to Kushner-Witkoff-Rowan). PIF's continuity is a feature of the GCC capital-recycling structure rather than evidence of continuity-by-successor. The continuity hypothesis is supported here by the fact that PIF as a payer did not lose its US elite access when Epstein died; it simply migrated to a different set of intermediaries who are now in or adjacent to the administration.

## What is missing from the continuity hypothesis

In the spirit of stating gaps explicitly:

- **No documented trafficking continuity** has surfaced from the Epstein network to the Trump-family or Kushner financial operations. The Pierce email is direct evidence of Pierce's Epstein-era communications, not of post-Epstein-death trafficking activity by Pierce or by the Trump family.
- **No recovered Epstein "client list" or "little black book"** has been released. The Trump DOJ has explicitly held back FBI witness interview records (Ro Khanna noting that survivors named other men whose names have not been released).
- **No documented blackmail-infrastructure successor** has been identified. Whether anyone currently operates the kind of camera-equipped property network Epstein operated is unknown. (Note Jay Puckett's framing 2026-06-12: "we do not know if he does or doesnt have cameras, likely does. maybe he doesnt traffic kids we do not know yet.")
- **Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent is blocking** Senator Wyden's access to the Treasury records that would document the precise wire transfers ($1.1 billion via 4,725 wires through one Epstein account, plus "hundreds of millions" via Russian correspondent banks per Wyden). The Senate Republicans blocked Wyden's "Produce Epstein Treasury Records Act" in March 2026. If those records are surrendered, they would be the dispositive document set for testing this entire hypothesis.
- **No documented direct Witkoff-Epstein link** has surfaced. Witkoff is in the same NYC real estate / political circle as Trump and many Epstein-adjacent figures; he started his career at Dreyer & Traub when Trump was a client. The absence of a direct documented link is itself worth recording.

## What would change the picture

Any one of the following would materially shift the hypothesis status:

1. Release of the FBI witness interview records currently being withheld by the DOJ
2. Treasury surrender of the full Wyden-requested records (4,725 wires, Russian correspondent banks)
3. Leak or court-ordered release of the Affinity Partners Parallel Fund I LP foreign beneficial owners list (currently being subpoenaed by Garcia/Wyden)
4. Disclosure of WLF foreign-source LP details under the Khanna investigation
5. Documented Kushner-to-Epstein direct correspondence beyond the existing Ambani-broker pattern
6. Any documented Witkoff-to-Epstein direct link
7. Any documented current-era operator-of-properties-with-cameras parallel to the Epstein Manhattan/Palm Beach/USVI/NM operations
8. Any documented payment from a previously identified Epstein payer (Wexner family, Black/Apollo, Pritzker, Hoffman, etc.) to a current Trump-family entity (Affinity, WLF, Trump Org, Truth Social, golf properties, the 1789 Capital vehicle, et al.)

The eighth point above is the central methodological test of this category: identify a name that paid Epstein, find that same name on the current Trump-family payment ledger, and the continuity hypothesis moves from structural pattern to documented same-payer continuity.
