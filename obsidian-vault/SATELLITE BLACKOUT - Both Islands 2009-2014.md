## The Technique: Resolution Masking

This is NOT a removal of imagery. It's a resolution ceiling.
- At low zoom: island exists on the map
- At high zoom: detail disappears
- Same technique as Kyl-Bingaman Amendment (Israel imagery restricted to 2m resolution)
- Same technique used for NGA Persistent Imaging Location List (60-70 classified locations worldwide)
