# "BELONGS TO INTELLIGENCE", Source Analysis

## The Claim

> "I was told Epstein 'belonged to intelligence' and to leave it alone."
>
>, Alexander Acosta, during Trump presidential transition vetting

## Source Chain

This statement is **secondhand**. The chain is:

```
Unknown person ("I was told")
    ↓ told
Alexander Acosta (US Attorney, SDFL)
    ↓ told
Trump transition interviewers
    ↓ reported in
Three EFTA documents (EFTA00076059, EFTA00076060, EFTA00097444)
    ↓ reported by
Daily Beast article (published Jul 9, 2019)
    ↓ forwarded to
Geoffrey Berman (US Attorney, SDNY), "For our next discussion"
```

## What We Know

- Acosta said it. It's documented in three separate EFTA releases.
- He was never asked under oath: **Who told you?**
- The Trump transition team accepted it as sufficient and confirmed his nomination.
- The Senate confirmed him 60-38.
- He served as Secretary of Labor for two years.
- He resigned July 2019 after the Miami Herald made the NPA a national scandal.

## What We Don't Know

- **Who gave the instruction.** No name has ever been attached.
- **Which intelligence service.** CIA? Mossad? NSA? DIA? "Intelligence" is deliberately vague.
- **In what form.** Was it a phone call? A meeting? A memo? A whisper in a hallway?
- **On whose authority.** Who authorized telling a US Attorney to drop a child sex trafficking case?
- **Whether it was true.** Did Epstein actually "belong to intelligence," or was the claim itself a protection mechanism, a magic phrase designed to make prosecutors back off without requiring proof?

## The Berman Forwarding

On July 9, 2019, three days after arresting Epstein, someone sent SDNY US Attorney Geoffrey Berman the Daily Beast article containing Acosta's claim, with the note: **"For our next discussion."**

The prosecutor who arrested Epstein was being briefed on the intelligence protection claim. This means:
1. SDNY was aware of the "belongs to intelligence" claim when they arrested him
2. They arrested him ANYWAY, suggesting SDNY either didn't believe it, didn't care, or had authorization that superseded it
3. Someone in Berman's circle thought it was important enough to flag for discussion

## The FBI CHS Corroboration

A separate October 2020 FBI Confidential Human Source document states:
> "Epstein was close to the former Prime Minister of Israel, Ehud Barak, and trained as a spy under him."

This is INDEPENDENT of Acosta's claim. Different source. Different time. Same conclusion: Epstein had intelligence connections. The CHS adds specificity that Acosta's vague "intelligence" lacks, it names Israel, names Barak, and describes a training relationship.

## Tier Classification

- **Acosta's claim:** Tier 1, documented in EFTA, never retracted, but secondhand
- **The identity of who told Acosta:** Unknown, no one has asked under oath
- **FBI CHS corroboration:** Tier 1 as a document, Tier 2 as content (single source, unverified)
- **The instruction still being active in 2019:** Tier 2, inferred from the "Pending Inactive" status and pattern of non-prosecution

## The Question Nobody Has Asked

Alexander Acosta has never been asked under oath: **Who told you Epstein belonged to intelligence?**

This is the single most important unanswered question in the case. The answer identifies the person or agency that authorized the protection of a child sex trafficker. Every other question, who killed him, who cleaned the Gmail, who turned off the cameras, flows downstream from this one.

**Who gave the order?**
