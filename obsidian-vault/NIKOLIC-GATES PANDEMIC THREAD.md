## SECTION 1: THE 2017 PANDEMIC INVESTMENT EMAILS

### 1A. The May 24, 2017 Email (Nikolic-Epstein-Gates)

On **May 24, 2017**, Boris Nikolic sent an email addressed to both Jeffrey Epstein and Bill Gates regarding a proposed donor-advised fund (DAF) concept. The email thread discussed a Gates-JPMorgan charitable giving platform.

Nikolic wrote: **"It might be a great path forward for some key areas such as Energy, pandemic etc."**

Key observations:
- "Pandemic" was listed as a **standing investment category** -- equivalent to energy -- suitable for long-term private capital mobilization
- The proposed DAF structure was designed to aggregate global capital with donor anonymity
- Embedded examples included vaccine purchasing, disease surveillance infrastructure, and cross-border health initiatives
- Epstein had framed DAFs as a "counter balance" to anticipated cuts in public science funding

### 1B. The "bgc3 Deliverables and Scope" Document (March 3, 2017)

A document dated **March 3, 2017** titled "bgc3 Deliverables and Scope" involved Bill Gates, Larry Cohen, and Jeffrey Epstein. It outlined five points of proposed work for bgC3 (Gates' private strategic office, now known as Gates Ventures):

1. Domestic health
2. Personal health data infrastructure
3. Neurotechnology
4. Brain science
5. **"Follow-up recommendations and/or technical specifications for strain pandemic simulation"**

Pandemic simulation was treated as a **technical discipline** within the same planning universe as health surveillance, data systems, and neurotechnology defense applications.

### 1C. January 2017 iMessage Thread (Epstein's Phone)

A January 2017 iMessage thread recovered from Epstein's phone shows an associate listing **"pandemics (just did pandemic simulation)"** as a professional credential while simultaneously discussing career placement into:
- Gates' private office
- Boris Nikolic's Biomatics Capital
- **Merck's vaccine team**
- **Swiss Re's pandemic reinsurance products**
- The World Economic Forum

This demonstrates Epstein was actively **placing personnel** into pandemic-related institutions across the private sector, reinsurance industry, pharmaceutical companies, and international governance bodies simultaneously.

### 1D. The March 2015 "Preparing for Pandemics" Email

An email dated **March 19, 2015** titled "Preparing for Pandemics" discussed:
- A planned meeting on pandemic preparedness
- "Next steps" and formal involvement of the **World Health Organization (WHO)** and the **International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC)**
- Explicit discussion of **"co-branding"** pandemic preparedness with these institutions

The conference was organized by Terje Rod-Larsen (Norwegian diplomat, former President of the International Peace Institute) and included:
- Margaret Chan (Director General of WHO)
- Peter Maurer (President of ICRC)
- Michael Moller (acting Head of the UN Office in Geneva)
- Kevin Rudd (former PM of Australia)

## SECTION 2: THE GATES-EPSTEIN-NIKOLIC RELATIONSHIP TIMELINE

### Phase 1: Introduction and Cultivation (2009-2011)

- **2009:** Melanie Walker -- a Gates Foundation senior advisor and neurosurgeon -- introduced Boris Nikolic to Jeffrey Epstein. Nikolic was then serving as Gates' Chief Science Advisor. This was **one year after Epstein's 2008 conviction** for soliciting sex from minors.
- **December 7, 2009:** Epstein wrote "We are going to have fun" in response to Nikolic making a list of rising star scientists they "should visit together."
- **2009-2010:** Epstein immediately "took Nikolic under his wing, urging his new friend to start managing Gates' biotech investments" (STAT News).
- **January 31, 2011:** Gates and Epstein met face-to-face for the first time at Epstein's Manhattan townhouse. Nikolic facilitated this introduction.
- **May 2011:** Dinner meeting attended by Gates, Epstein, JPMorgan executive James Staley, former Treasury Secretary Lawrence Summers, and Boris Nikolic at Epstein's residence.

### Phase 2: Deep Entanglement (2011-2014)

- **2011-2014:** Gates met with Epstein at least six times (documented by NYT), mostly at Epstein's Manhattan townhouse.
- **Late 2012:** Epstein pitched Gates on a charitable vehicle (DAF) through which Gates would manage the wealth of newly-minted billionaires.
- **March 1, 2013:** Gates flew on Epstein's private jet from Teterboro to Palm Beach.
- **May 2013:** Nikolic -- then still at the Gates Foundation -- emailed an immigration attorney about Mila Antonova (see Section 5).
- **July 2013:** Gates wrote to Nikolic: "We should meet on Wednesday to discuss your job. There is going to have to be a transition. I feel very bad about it but I don't see a way around it."
- **July 11, 2013:** Gates emailed Epstein to say that Larry Cohen (Gates' scheduler at bgC3) was authorized to speak with Epstein about Nikolic's employment.
- **2013:** Documents show Gates "specifically requested" Epstein to "personally serve as the representative" of Nikolic in negotiations over the termination of his work with Gates. An earlier draft of the resignation announcement was **written by Epstein** and sent to Nikolic and Cohen.
- **September 2013:** Nikolic exited the Gates Foundation with a **$5 million advance**.
- **2014:** Nikolic and Epstein saw Nikolic's volatile exit as at least partly Melinda Gates' decision.

### Phase 3: Biomatics Capital and Continued Contact (2016-2019)

- **2016:** Nikolic co-founded **Biomatics Capital** in Seattle with Julie Sunderland (former director of Program Related Investments at the Gates Foundation).
- **2017:** The pandemic investment email thread (see Section 1).
- **January 2017:** Walker iMessaged Epstein that Gates "feels bad about the DAF btw." Gates "thought great idea but wife wouldn't allow."
- **2017:** Epstein's blackmail pressure on Gates intensified (see Section 5).
- **2018:** Biomatics raised **$300 million** for its second healthcare fund.
- **March 2019:** Nikolic wrote to Epstein: "Please let me know when you know. Would love to see you. How are you?" -- demonstrating active, friendly communication five months before Epstein's arrest.
- **August 8, 2019:** Epstein signed his will, naming Nikolic as alternate executor.
- **August 10, 2019:** Epstein died in custody.
- **Post-August 2019:** Nikolic resigned from boards, was forced to unwind Biomatics Capital, and the industry distanced itself from him.

### Phase 4: Comeback (2024-2026)

- **2024-2025:** Alexis Borisy (Curie.Bio) helped Nikolic launch a new fund and raise **more than $100 million**.
- **March 2026:** STAT News reported on the comeback: "Epstein's 'great friend' makes a comeback in the biotech VC world."
- **2026:** Multiple VCs told STAT they "would not have done business with Nikolic because of his documented links to Epstein."

### 3B. CEPI (Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations)

CEPI was formally launched in **January 2017** at the World Economic Forum in Davos -- the same month and year as the Epstein pandemic simulation iMessage and months before the Nikolic-Epstein-Gates pandemic DAF email.

- Co-founded by: Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, Wellcome Trust, governments of India and Norway, World Economic Forum

- Mission: Accelerate development of vaccines against emerging infectious diseases
- When COVID-19 hit, CEPI "responded immediately" and built "one of the world's largest and most diverse portfolios of COVID-19 vaccine candidates"
- 2022: Gates Foundation pledged additional $150M (total with Wellcome: $300M)

### 3C. Event 201 (October 18, 2019)

Two months after Epstein's death and just weeks before the first COVID-19 cases:
- Partnership of WEF, Gates Foundation, and Johns Hopkins

- Described as "a coordinated rehearsal of a rapid vaccine deployment system"
- Built by CEPI infrastructure, financed in part by the Gates Foundation

### 3D. Swiss Re Pandemic Reinsurance

Epstein's network was placing personnel into **Swiss Re's pandemic reinsurance unit**, where "parametric triggers" automatically pay out when a pandemic is declared. This represents a financial instrument that profits from pandemic declarations -- a direct monetization path.
